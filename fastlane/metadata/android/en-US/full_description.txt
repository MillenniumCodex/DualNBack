Train your working memory and boost your fluid intelligence with Dual N-Back. Based on a scientifically proven cognitive task, this app provides a clean and minimalist way to engage in effective brain training shown in studies to increase IQ.

The dual n-back task challenges you to track two separate sequences of information simultaneously, improving your ability to focus, manage distractions, and manipulate information in your head.

Features:
* Adjustable N-level to scale the difficulty to your current ability.
* Customizable number of rounds for each game session.
* Track your progress over time with clear win rates and score charts.
* Simple pause and resume functionality for training on the go.

This application is 100% free and open-source. It contains absolutely no ads, no trackers, and requires no permissions.
